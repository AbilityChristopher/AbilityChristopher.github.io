Ser-Av Foundation – Strategy Refinement

Role: Lead facilitator & strategic analyst

Challenge: The organisation faced inefficiencies in three key areas: operations, institutional partnerships, and human resources.

Key Gaps Identified

  • Operations: Communication breakdowns and high financial costs
  • Institutions: Lack of reliable, long‑term partnerships
  • Human Resources: Shortage of personnel and expertise

Solution Approach

Led a two‑day strategy workshop using gap analysis and root‑cause mapping. Developed actionable recommendations for each area.

Impact

The team adopted new communication protocols, partnership criteria, and a phased recruitment plan. Early results showed a 30% reduction in operational friction.

NITDA DL4ALL – Digital Literacy for the Informal Sector

Role: Certified Facilitator

Goal: Bridge the digital divide by training informal workforce members in basic digital skills.

Activities

  • Organised community‑based training sessions
  • Taught internet navigation, online safety, and digital communication
  • Provided post‑training support and resource guides

Outcome

Trained over 120 participants across three local government areas. Many gained confidence to use mobile banking and apply for online jobs.

Youth Mentorship & Career Guidance

Role: Mentor & workshop facilitator

Context: Many secondary school leavers in Benue State lack career direction and mentorship.

Approach

  • Conducted career assessment sessions
  • Led group discussions on goal setting and skill development
  • Connected participants with online learning resources

Results

Over 50 students received personalised guidance. Feedback showed increased clarity about future education and career choices.
