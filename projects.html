01
💻 NITDA DL4ALL Initiative

Digital Literacy for All

📍 Benue State, Nigeria

Role Certified DL4ALL Facilitator
Period 2025 – 2026
Focus Digital inclusion for informal sector workers

The NITDA Digital Literacy For All (DL4ALL) programme is a federal government-backed initiative to bring basic digital skills to Nigerians who have been left behind by the digital economy — particularly those in the informal sector who use feature phones, run small businesses, or have never received formal digital training.

As a certified facilitator, I designed and delivered practical, jargon-free workshops that met people where they were — covering everything from basic smartphone use to mobile banking, online safety, and using digital tools for everyday life and business.

What I Did
  • Delivered structured digital literacy workshops to individuals in the informal sector
  • Covered basic ICT: smartphone navigation, email, internet browsing, and online communication
  • Facilitated sessions on mobile banking, digital payments, and financial app safety
  • Taught online scam awareness, data privacy, and digital security basics
  • Introduced participants to social media for small business and personal branding
  • Adapted training delivery for participants with no prior digital exposure
500+ Individuals Trained

02
💰 KAFI Foundation

Financial Literacy Leadership Programme

📍 Benue State, Nigeria

Role Financial Literacy Fellow & Outreach Facilitator
Period 2025 – 2026
Focus Financial education for secondary school students

As a graduate of the KAFI Financial Literacy Leadership Fellowship, I participated in structured training on financial education, leadership development, and community financial empowerment — then took that knowledge into secondary schools across Benue State.

The gap is real: most Nigerian students leave secondary school without ever being taught how to manage money, understand income, or think about financial futures. My outreach sessions worked to begin closing that gap — one school at a time.

What I Did
  • Completed the Financial Literacy Leadership Fellowship with KAFI Foundation
  • Designed and delivered age-appropriate financial literacy sessions for secondary students
  • Taught budgeting, saving habits, and understanding income vs expenses
  • Facilitated discussions on financial goal-setting and long-term money thinking
  • Delivered sessions on avoiding financial scams, debt traps, and peer pressure spending
  • Introduced basic entrepreneurship and income generation concepts
100+ Students Reached

04
🩸 Community Health Outreach

Menstrual Hygiene Management Sensitisation

📍 Kanshio & Wadata, Benue State

Role Sensitisation Facilitator
Period 2026
Reach 4 schools across 2 communities

Menstrual health remains a deeply stigmatised topic in many Nigerian communities — with girls missing school, facing shame, and lacking access to accurate health information. This outreach programme took the conversation directly into primary and secondary schools in two communities in Benue State.

As a facilitator, I created a safe, educational space for students, teachers, and community members to engage honestly with menstrual health — breaking silence, reducing stigma, and equipping girls with knowledge and confidence.

What I Did
  • Delivered MHM sensitisation sessions at LGEA Primary School, Kanshio
  • Facilitated outreach at UBE Kanshio and NKST Primary School, Wadata
  • Conducted awareness sessions at UBE Wadata
  • Led interactive learning sessions with pupils, students, and teachers
  • Addressed stigma, hygiene practices, and basic menstrual health facts
  • Created space for questions, discussion, and peer-to-peer learning
4 Schools Reached
2 Communities

🌱 Independent Initiative

Youth Career Guidance & Mentorship
