About Me

I am a purpose-driven leader, educator, digital literacy advocate, and financial literacy advocate passionate about unlocking human potential and helping individuals build meaningful, impactful lives.

With a background in Guidance and Counselling, I am committed to empowering young people and communities through mentorship, education, leadership development, career guidance, and transformational speaking.

My work focuses on: Digital Literacy Training, Financial Literacy Education, Youth Empowerment, Personal Development, Leadership Development, Career Guidance & Counselling, Public Speaking & Communication, Community Impact Initiatives.

I currently serve under the NYSC programme where I actively facilitate digital literacy education through the NITDA DL4ALL initiative, helping bridge the digital divide among individuals in the informal sector.

I am also passionate about expanding financial literacy awareness for students and young professionals through educational outreach and advocacy initiatives. My long-term vision is to build platforms, programs, and initiatives that transform lives through education, leadership, technology, and purpose discovery.
