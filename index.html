About Me

Purpose built through service.

I am Ability Christopher — a Guidance and Counselling professional, youth development advocate, and emerging voice in education, digital inclusion, and financial literacy in Nigeria.

My work is simple: help individuals — especially young people — move from confusion to clarity. Whether through one-on-one counselling, classroom education, community workshops, or a stage, I show up with one goal: to unlock the potential already inside the people I serve.

I hold a B.Ed in Guidance and Counselling (Second Class Upper) and am currently deepening my real-world impact through the NYSC programme, facilitating digital literacy education and youth empowerment in Benue State.
