God Gave You Potential ❤️

📅 23 May 2026 • 4 min read

When God created human beings, He spoke to Himself. You came forth from God because He planned that we would be spirit – just as He is Spirit.

Yet too many people never discover God’s purpose for creating them. They search for meaning in achievements, possessions, and the approval of others… but not in the One who designed them.

The result? Lives that feel unfulfilled. Dreams that never take shape. Potential that remains buried.

But here’s the truth:
You were born with divine potential – placed inside you by the Creator Himself. Your purpose isn’t something you invent; it’s something you discover in Him.

Don’t waste the gift God placed within you. Start seeking His purpose today.

Inspired by the timeless words of Dr. Myles Munroe in “Understanding Your Potential.”
If you haven’t read it yet, add it to your list – it’s a life-changing guide to unlocking what God has already placed inside you.

Take a moment today to reflect:
Am I living out the potential God placed in me, or have I settled for less than His purpose?

Let’s rise to the level of the potential Heaven invested in us.
