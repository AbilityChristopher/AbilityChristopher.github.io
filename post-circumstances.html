Dream Past Your Circumstances

📅 25 May 2026 • 3 min read

Remaining a victim of circumstance is a state of being that we choose – a choice that:

  • 01. Allows us to blame other people
  • 02. Let us blame circumstances
  • 03. Permits us to avoid responsibility for our actions
  • 04. Causes us to feel sorry for ourselves
  • 05. Guarantee that we continue to act as victims.

I know it's easier said than done, I know.
But you've got to encourage yourself today, not tomorrow.
Take a step that your future self will thank you for.
And trust God for the outcome.
