🩸 Menstrual Hygiene Management Awareness Initiative

As part of my community development engagement during service year, I participated in an MHM sensitization program organized through Ser-Av Foundation in collaboration with partner organizations. The outreach was conducted for students of LGEA Primary School and Universal Basic Education (UBE) Kanshio, Makurdi, Benue State, promoting menstrual health awareness, personal hygiene education, and informed health practices among young learners. This initiative contributed to meaningful conversations around health education, student wellbeing, and youth empowerment.
